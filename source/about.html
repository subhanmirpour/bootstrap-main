About Us: Durham College Students' Research on the Canadian Automotive Industry

We are a group of Durham College students dedicated to exploring the impact of automation and robotics on the Canadian automotive industry. As the sector transitions to electric vehicle (EV) production, we aim to inform stakeholders and investors about how advanced technologies can improve manufacturing efficiency, enhance competitiveness, and support the industry's growth. Through our research, we are highlighting solutions to the challenges faced by automotive manufacturers, particularly the balance between internal combustion engine (ICE) and EV production lines. By advocating for the adoption of automation, we aim to help Canadian companies thrive in a rapidly evolving market.

Our Mission: Driving Innovation in the Canadian Automotive Sector

  • Addressing the Transition to EVs

    We explore the challenges automotive manufacturers face in balancing traditional ICE vehicle production with the rapid rise of EV demand and production.

  • Advocating for Advanced Automation

    Our research shows that automation and robotics are key solutions for enhancing production efficiency and ensuring flexibility in the production of both ICE and EV vehicles.
